Cross-promotion is a form of marketing where you partner with another business to both promote your product to their audience and to promote their product to your audience.
A broker is an intermediary, an agent that facilitate deals between parties previously unknown to each other.

Cross-Promotion Broker is here to help you find a trustable business with similar audiences and create cross-promotion campaigns with them.

How does it work?

You register your business with us using the form below explaining your business and audience.
Other business owners do the same.
We match you with the most promising partners and ask each one if you are interested in creating a cross-promotion campaign together.
If (and only if) both sides accept, we charge a fee and introduce you both.
You plan the cross-promotion campaign(s) together, in the form that you prefer and launch it!

The fee and the rules

After the proposal mutual acceptance and before the introduction we charge a fee for our services. The amount is proportional to the size of your partner's audience, so each introduction has a different price. Once you accept the proposal, we show you the price so you decide if you will go on with the transaction.

The rules
All campaigns must be two-sided promotional exchanges, i.e. launched by both parties to the respective audiences.
The campaigns won't involve any monetary exchange between the two business partners. The value of cross-promotion comes from marketing benefits to the business only.

Why not cut the middleman?

Well, actually, you should!
More so, we recommend you to do it by yourself. That way you will learn first hand the great value of cross-promotion marketing and when you exhaust your personal network and lack the time to thoroughly look for more potential partners you will come to us.
We always find easier to convince to use our services those who already did cross-promotion before.
